what we organize for you

As a part of our curriculum, we organize a broad range of workshops and events - technical and non-technical to keep our participants engaged with the latest technologies and hacks.

Under the Hood

Build a pc workshop - where Computer Hardware enthusiasts get to build their own personal monster.

Web Craft

A hands-on workshop organized where students get a chance to get familiar with the web technologies.

Riddler

An mind-buzzing online cryptic event that will for sure make you scratch your heads. Plus, it's fun.

MohMAYA - 3D development

A hands-on session on 3D development using Autodesk Maya to create and use 3D objects in game development.

Laser Tag

A thrilling, adrenaline - pumping, team play event. We have transported a virtual game to reality via this event.

Yuvana

Yuvana is an event that tests the student's coding ability under high stress and competition.

CODESPACE

Learn, build and explore software and hardware with participants from all domains.
A national level hackathon that enables the thinkers and doers to reach out to certain communities which suffer from lack of basic amenities, with an aim to hone technology and creativity.
Visit codespace.csivit.com
